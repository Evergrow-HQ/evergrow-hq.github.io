Evergrow is derisking decarbonization.

Carbon developers often have highly variable revenue streams because they are paid in carbon credits.

Evergrow provides credit price downside protection - this allows carbon developers to achieve favorable rates from lenders and unlock additional capital for decarbonization.

Traditional option structures can provide similar types of downside protection - but traditional options are expensive because they pay out whenever the price drops below the strike price.

We at Evergrow provide a variant of a put option that guarantees average revenue e.g. on an annual or quarterly basis, the frequency at which they pay out to their lenders. Through this averagining methodology, it is possible to have a much cheaper option. This is called an Asian option.
