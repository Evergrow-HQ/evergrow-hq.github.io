Evergrow makes climate funding accessible

Our software underwrites compliance and expedites offtake for climate tax credits to make this funding easily accessible to decarbonization projects

The Inflation Reduction Act of 2022 is a game changer for American climate policy. New funding available for solar, wind, batteries, carbon capture, sustainable aviation fuel, hydrogen, nuclear, and green manufacturing will spur massive new development of decarbonization infrastructure.
However, this new climate funding is complex to access. It is given in the form of tax credits, which require specialized offtake or equity. It also comes with significant compliance burden, in particular requiring every contractor and subcontractor to document that they've paid prevailing wages and hired apprentices.
Evergrow makes it simple for everyone in the project value chain to comply with these rules and access the funding. Our solution handles IRA compliance, underwriting, and offtake to ensure that projects can get building fast.
